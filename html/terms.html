Description of Subscription Services

sprouting threads is an internet based subscription service through which subscribed parents receive a shipment (“box”) of pre-selected clothes for their child to match their unique style profile created by the subscriber. Each box contains a preselected quantity/assortment within a preselected frequency determined by the subscriber. A sprouting threads account and child profile is required in order to take advantage of the subscription service.


Subscription Availability

Subscription eligibility and availability is limited to the United States and certain U.S. Territories (e.g. Puerto Rico).


Subscription Service Options

There are different subscription service level options (frequency, quantity, and assortment) available to You. Frequency is how often you wish to receive deliveries (monthly or seasonally). Quantity is the number of items per shipment. Assortment is whether you prefer all new clothes or a “blend box” that contains an assortment of new and “used” clothes from our reThreads program. Used items are defined by our used guidelines posted on reThreads page. Your choice of service level options will affect your subscription fees. For more details about the Subscription Service please see FAQ page.


sprouting threads Seasonal Subscriptions operate upon the following seasonal schedule:

Winter: February 1st – April 30th
Spring: May 1st – July 31st
Summer: August – October
Winter: November – January
