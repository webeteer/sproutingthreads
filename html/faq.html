General Questions


What is sprouting threads?

At sprouting threads we provide parents a web enabled service to find just the right clothes to express their little ones unique style. We are a subscription based clothing service that gives the members options of frequency, quantity & assortment to meet their family’s needs. We offer members high quality boutique kids lines and an option to reThread outgrown clothes.


Signing Up


How do I join sprouting threads?

It’s easy! Just click on “Ready, Set, Go!” This will prompt you to pick a style that best suits your child, complete a simple profile quiz, then checkout!


I’ve signed up! What should I expect?

You should expect a confirmation email and an awesome package coming your way shortly!


When will I get my first box?

If you signed up after the cutoff date (5th of the month), we will start your first box the following month. If you have signed up for a seasonal box you will have the option of starting right away or waiting until the following season. There is a season calendar to give you the details for each season’s months. Please visit subscription details for more information.


But I have more than one child! How do I get multiple boxes delivered?

After completing the profile of your first child you will get the option to Add child. When you add additional children you can complete their profile & subscription details independently however shipments dates will be synchronized.


What is rethreading anyway?

It is a clothing recycle program we offer for our members to receive credits towards future purchases, when their children out grow their threads. Members can also opt to purchase reThreads in their orders by selecting the blended subscription option. We provide you a prepaid shipping label/bag with every order to use to ship anything reThreads back to us at anytime. Check out our reThread page for more details and to get started.


Can I purchase reThreads only?

At this time, we can only offer a blend, however as our inventories build we look forward to offering this option.



Member Profile


What if my child has a growth spurt or their style changes? How do I change this?

Yes you can, login to member account and change the sizing. Please allow 15 days for any changes to take effect.


Shipping


Do I have to pay for shipping? And when can I expect to receive my boxes?

Shipping is FREE! Our boxes ship the 10th of the month. When your box ships, we will send you an email notification with your tracking information.


Where does sprouting threads ship?

We ship to all 50 states within the U.S. and U.S. Territories. Unfortunately, we don't ship to Canada or to other international addresses. Yet.


Can I change my shipping address or billing info?

Of course! Just login to sprouting threads and go to member account. From there, you can edit your shipping address, billing info, etc. Please allow 15 days for any changes to take effect.



Billing


When will I be billed? How do renewals work?

For self-subscriptions you will be charged your monthly or seasonal rate on the 1st of the month. You will be auto renewed, unless otherwise specified, paused or cancelled according to our policies. Any changes to the subscription package can be done at any time, but must be complete within 15 days of the first of the month in order to go into effect the following month.

Gift subscriptions (purchased through “Gift”) are purchased for a set price and do not automatically renew. If you would like to renew a gift subscription contact us and we will make it happen. If you'd like to renew your own subscription after receiving a gift, simply purchase a new subscription for yourself through “Ready, Set, Go?” , it will run you through the easy process.



Gifting


Can I gift a sprouting threads subscription?

It’s easy and a great present! Just click on “Gift”, review the subscription options and select a $ you would like to gift and then go through the checkout process. We will email you a confirmation along with sending the giftee an electronic gift card to redeem. They’ll have the opportunity to customize the profile, as well as enter their shipping information. Once the gift code is redeemed, we’ll start shipping their boxes!


Other


What is your cancellation policy?

We hope you never leave us, but you can cancel by sending us a note or contacting us. If we receive you cancellation BEFORE the 1st of the month (11:59pm EST on the last day of the previous month), your cancellation will take effect the following month.

Can I put my subscription on pause?

Sure! Just contact us by the 1st of the month and tell us how long you would like to pause, and we’ll be happy to delay your shipment.


What is your return policy?

Please visit your member account page to complete the return form and return them within 10 days of receiving your package. Items must have the original sprouting threads tag attached. We have provided you a prepaid shipping label/bag to use for easy returns. Your items will be credited to your account and may be used for future shipments. Credits will be applied for each item to your sprouting threads account.

I have a boutique clothing line that I think would be great on your site, How can I submit my product for evaluation?

We love hearing from new companies! Please e-mail us at info@sproutingthreads.com to submit the products you would like us to consider, please include product detail/lookbook along with wholesale pricing. We’ll contact you if we are interested to ask for more information or samples.
